About Sigma Coffee

Coffee is our passion. As one of Australis's most highly regarded specialty roasters, we've always had one simple goal: creating the best and most sustainable coffee in the world.

Mission

Since our passionate founder YiFan Feng opened his first Sigma cafe in China in 2000, we've been on a mission to help everyone love great coffee as much as we do. We believe in coffee's ability to inspire genuine optimism and joy. From your very first sip things magically get better; conversations flow freely,grand ideas flourish and new connections feel like old frineds.
